Frequent Questions

You just have to select all the products you want to purchase. Next, in the shopping cart, to find out the shipping cost, place your postal code in the corresponding box, choose the messaging service of your choice and below select the payment method. Then you click on the BUY button and you can access as a customer (if you already have a Purchase Gamer account) or create a new customer. Finally, you complete the steps provided by the wizard, until you confirm the purchase. You will be assigned an order number and the details of the order will be displayed. We will also send an email to your registered email with the details of the order placed.

All prices on the web include VAT, and are expressed in Argentine pesos.

We have two forms of payment: through deposit/bank transfer, with which you get the special price, or through the Gamer Payment methods (Visa or MasterCard) or MercadoPago (Online Cards, PagoFácil and RapiPago) with the which you can pay in installments, at the list price.

Once the order is placed, we provide you with the CBU data. You must pay and inform the payment from our website, before the expiration date of the reservation.

It is an exclusive method of Gamma Tech Shop, to pay online through Visa and Mastercard, with which you can access 3 and 12 installments without interest if you use a credit card provided by a bank.

You can do it in three ways: With online cards in installments (you cannot access installments without interest); Through RapiPago/PagoFácil (you pay at the list price, but you can't make installments, you can only pay in one payment); and making a transfer from your MercadoPago account

First of all, to know the cost of shipping, once you add your purchase to the cart, you just have to place your postal code in the corresponding box, select the courier of your choice and choose if you want the withdrawal at a branch or the delivery to home. We currently ship nationwide through Oca and Andreani; and if you are in CABA or surroundings, you can select the Private Messaging service that is exclusive to Compra Gamer. Keep in mind that, to calculate the shipping cost, both the dimensions and weight of the package and the distance from the delivery location are taken into consideration.

In all purchases made on the web, we provide, without exception, the purchase invoice. Once you place and pay for the order, we send the corresponding invoice to your email address. Of course, you can also download it from the My account < My invoices section. In case you need invoice A, you only have to enter your CUIT when loading the order through the web. Keep in mind that invoice A may have perceptions.

To make queries/claims related to the guarantee or return of any of your purchases, at the end of this section we have the section “Compra Gamer helps you. What is your question?" where you must expose your case, selecting the reason for "After-sales" that suits your requirement and one of our representatives will offer you the corresponding information on how to proceed.

All redemptions of games and/or benefits are made after the delivery of the product and are completely digital. The exchanges are made from HERE with the data of the purchase invoice and the email registered in the purchase. The valid date for the validity of the promotions is taken as the day the order was created and not the date of payment or the date of issue of the invoice. Consider that, if you made the purchase of the promotional product, in a timely manner, but cannot make the exchange within the date, this is because there is no stock of codes. Also, know that the availability of codes is limited, and once they run out, it will not be possible to redeem them.
